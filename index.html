Brennan Mackay About Me

Hi, I'm a software developer from Los Angeles with an interest in media and networks. I finished up my undergrad with a BSc in Computer Science and Music in June from the University of Victoria. My courses focused on software development, large scale networks and multimedia systems, signal processing and compression, acoustics and audio projects, and music information retrieval. I am learning web design and have a strong knowledge of python, C, java and SQL. This site is home to some of my projects - you can find some others on github here.
